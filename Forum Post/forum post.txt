[notice][size=150][color=HotPink]/[/color][b]/[/b] [color=HotPink][b]Qualifiers[/color][/b][/size]

[list]
[*]The first stage of the tournament is the Qualifier.
[*]Players sign up for any one of the lobbies shown on the Mainsheet.
[*]Should a Player miss their lobby, they may choose a different lobby that still has space.
[*]If none of the lobbies work for a Player, they may schedule an extra lobby at a time that suits them. Please be aware that this option depends entirely on staff availability.
    During these lobbies, each Player will play through all maps in order from NM1 to DT2.
[*]If a player disconnects during the first 30 seconds of a map, an abort can be requested to replay the map. There will be no 3rd try, in case of another disconnect. If a disconnect occurs afte the first 30 seconds of a map, the player(s) that disconnected can replay the map at the end of their Qualifiers lobby.
[*]If one or more Players in the lobby do not want to replay a certain map, they will just wait outside the lobby for the other Players to finish.
[/list]


[size=150][color=HotPink]/[/color][b]/[/b] [color=HotPink][b]Match Procedure[/color][/b][/size]

[list]
[*]In the discord, the referee will ping both Players [color=HotPink][b]10-15 minutes[/b][/color] prior to the scheduled match start.
[quote]Should this not happen until 5 minutes before match start, please message the Admins/Host ASAP![/quote]
[*][color=HotPink][b]Match invites[/b][/color] will be send [color=HotPink][b]5 - 10 minutes[/b][/color] prior to match start.
[*]Playere have up to [color=HotPink][b]10 minutes past scheduled time[/b][/color] to join the lobby. Is either Player still missing after warmups, they Player will [color=HotPink][b]lose by default[/b][/color]. Should both Players fail to show up, [color=HotPink][b]higher seed will win by default[/b][/color].
[*]The first Player in the lobby  is [color=red][b]Team Red and plays in Slot 1[/b][/color]. The other Player is [color=blue][b]Team Blue, playing in Slot 2[/b][/color].
[*][color=HotPink][b]NoFail[/b][/color] will be [color=HotPink][b]enforced[/b][/color] for every map.
[*]Each Player has the option of [color=HotPink][b]1 warmup[/b][/color] map. This map shall not be longer than [color=HotPink][b]4:00 minutes[/b][/color] and [color=HotPink][b]cannot be a map from the pool[/b][/color]. Warmups are played with Freemod ON.
[*]If your matchup is streamed, please send your warmup to the referee, [color=HotPink][b]30 minutes prior[/b][/color] to match start.
[*]After warmups are completed, both [color=HotPink][b]captains will !roll[/b][/color] for Ban/Pick phase.
[*]The time for banning or picking a map is [color=HotPink][b]90 seconds[/b][/color]. After a pick was called, there will be a [color=HotPink][b]120 second timer to get ready[/b][/color].
[*][color=HotPink][b]Double Picking and Double Banning are not allowed![/b][/color]
[*]Should a player disconnect [color=HotPink][b]within the first 30 seconds[/b][/color] of a map, the map will be [color=HotPink][b]aborted[/b][/color]. Should a player disconnect after [color=HotPink][b]30 seconds[/b][/color], their score will count as 0 unless the score is provided.
[quote]Aborts only happen once per Player, per match![/quote]
[*]Each Player has an [color=HotPink][b]optional 120-second timeout[/b][/color], once per match. This timeout may be called at any stage in the match after the first map has been played.
[*][b]FreeMod Rules:[/b] The following are the mod options/restrictions for each player
[list]
[*][b]Player 1: [color=yellow]HD [/color][/b]
[*][b]Player 2: [color=red]HR[/color]/[color=yellow]HD[/color][color=red]HR[/color]/[color=green]EZ[/color]/[color=green]EZ[/color][color=yellow]HD[/color]/[color=#CD7F32]FL[/color][/b]
[*][b]EZ modifier: x1.727[/b]
[quote]This DOES NOT apply for TieBreaker! NoMod or any mod combination is allowed![/quote]
[/list]
